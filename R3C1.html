Tulip chandelier

During an internship at Monolyt interior and product design in 2015, I designed the initial basis of the tulip chandelier for the Keukenhof restaurant the Blooming Dutch. After an ideation process involving sketching and making low fidelity mockups, a handcrafted prototype was made. Later on, iterative changes were made to the design by Monolyt to make the design more in line with the interior of the restaurant.
